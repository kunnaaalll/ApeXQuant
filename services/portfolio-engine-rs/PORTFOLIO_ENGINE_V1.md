## Objective
Build a deterministic portfolio management engine that owns account-level intelligence, global exposure, capital allocation, correlation management, and diversification.

## Architecture
- **State**: tracks margins, equity, and aggregate pnl.
- **Heat**: quantifies portfolio heat (0-100).
- **Allocation**: manages risk budget and max concurrent positions.
- **Drawdown**: peak-to-valley tracking and equity curve slope.
- **Correlation**: cluster detection and cross-symbol concentration.
